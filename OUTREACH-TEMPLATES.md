# Outreach Templates — TreeQuote

All templates use Mike's voice: start with "Hey", keep it short, lead with the point.
Fill-ins are in [brackets]. Source list: `/admin` Outreach tab or `contractor-lists/*.csv`.

---

## 1. Cold CALL script — contractor (~30 seconds)

> Hey, is this [First Name] with [Company]? This is Mike — I run a local site
> called TreeQuote where South Jersey homeowners request tree work quotes with
> photos and job details already filled in.
>
> I'm launching in [Turnersville / their town] and picking 10 founding
> companies. You'd get every matching lead **free** — no contract, no card.
> All I ask is you respond to leads within 24 hours and tell me what you think.
>
> After the free period it's pay-per-lead, around $10–20, and only for leads
> you choose to unlock — you see the whole job and the photos **before** paying.
>
> Want me to text you the link?

**Objection: "Is this like HomeAdvisor?"**
> Nope — that's why I built it. You see full job details AND photos before you
> pay anything, a lead never goes to more than 5 companies, and bogus leads get
> credited back, no argument. And for you it's free right now anyway.

**Objection: "I'm too busy."**
> That's exactly why it works — the homeowner fills everything out, you just
> look at the photos and decide if you want it. Takes a minute. I'll text you
> the link, look when you're free.

**Close:** get their cell, send Text #2 immediately.

## Rules of thumb (from GTM.md)

- Call 7–9am or 5–7pm — owners answer when crews aren't running.
- Target companies with 10–80 Google reviews (hungry but real). Skip the top 3.
- Always text the link after a call, answered or not.
- Cut anyone who ghosts two leads — a dead lead poisons the customer side.
- Never claim fake stats. "Founding launch" is the honest pitch.
